Schedule Guide

Running a school of ministry is hard work. Balancing the rigors of running a church and educating the next generation of ministers, it can be hard to find time for both. Below is a recommended time table for students which balances a robust prayer life, academics, and ministry work.

The schedule is based around abbreviated prayer times of the Rule of St. Benedict, a monk from the yth century who wanted to create an environment for people to grow closer to God and be engaged regularly in the work of prayer.
